## 📏 7. EFFORT ESTIMATION METHODOLOGY

### Feature Sizing (Planning Poker Indonesia Style)
| Size | T-Shirt | Hour Range | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| XS | 2-4 jam | Bug fix, copy tweak | Update harga di 1 halaman |
| S | 1 hari | Feature kecil | Tambah field di form |
| M | 2-3 hari | Feature sedang | Halaman baru dengan form |
| L | 1 minggu | Feature major | Pipeline CRM Kanban |
| XL | 2-3 minggu | Module baru | Auth + multi-tenant |
| XXL | >1 bulan | Product baru | Tower Private deployment |

**Rule:** Kalau estimasi XXL, **WAJIB break-down ke L** dulu sebelum mulai.
